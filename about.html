About

I’m Roland, an Interaction Designer from Detroit, MI.

I consider myself as an Interaction Designer with overlapping competencies in Design Research, User Experience & User Interface Design. The tools I have used to address design problems and realize design solutions include platforms like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, and Figma.

I’m a naturally empathetic person, and I value hearing people’s stories and experiences as it paints a picture of perspectives that I would otherwise be unexposed to. I believe my ability to take a design approach with empathy would make me great at visualizing products that best address the user’s needs.
